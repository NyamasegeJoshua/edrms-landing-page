What is EDRMS

Our government is transitioning to a paperless administration to enhance efficiency and reduce environmental impact. The Electronic Document and Records Management System (EDRMS) is central to this change, streamlining document management and improving transparency and accountability. Join us on this journey towards a sustainable, digital future.
