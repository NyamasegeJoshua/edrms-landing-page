What is EDRMS

Embracing the digital revolution, our government is transitioning towards a paperless administration. This transformation enhances efficiency, reduces environmental impact, and provides citizens with faster, more convenient access to services. Our Electronic Document and Records Management System (EDRMS) is at the heart of this change. It streamlines the creation, organization, and management of documents, ensuring a seamless flow of information. By automating processes, we’re not only saving time and resources but also improving transparency and accountability. Join us in this journey towards a sustainable, digital future.
